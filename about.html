MY

HOBBIES

Being a member of AKUD Mirce Acev since 2013 has been an incredible journey for me. This distinguished folk folklore ensemble, with its large and dedicated group of members, has allowed me to share the rich traditions of our music and dance with audiences around the world. We've traveled to numerous countries and cities, acting as cultural ambassadors and showcasing our vibrant and dynamic performances.

My time with AKUD Mirce Acev has been marked by unforgettable experiences and profound personal growth. Each performance is not just an opportunity to display our artistic talents but also a celebration of our heritage and the strong sense of community that binds us together. The ensemble provides a unique blend of entertainment and recreation, fostering camaraderie among members and creating lasting memories.

Through our international tours, I've had the chance to explore diverse cultures while proudly sharing my own. These shared experiences, whether during rehearsals, performances, or travels, have forged strong bonds and enriched my life with incredible moments that I will cherish for years to come.

Being part of AKUD Mirce Acev has given me a deeper appreciation for cultural arts and the joy they bring to audiences worldwide. It has also helped me develop my personal skills and play a role in preserving and promoting our traditional folk heritage on a global stage. I am grateful for the opportunity to be part of such a vibrant cultural and artistic society.
